Our Love Story

Sometimes, love finds its way through the most traditional paths — with a little twist of fate. Ours was an arranged match, but our story began much earlier, in a place close to both our hearts — Birgunj.

Aftab was living there to complete his +2 studies, and Shakina happened to be his neighbor. What started as casual hellos and occasional chats soon turned into a genuine friendship. We shared laughs, and countless cups of tea on the terrace — never imagining life would bring us together like this.

Years later, when our families brought up the proposal, it felt like destiny had simply circled back to what was always meant to be. There was no hesitation — just smiles, memories, and the joy of knowing that sometimes, the best surprises are the ones written long ago.

Now, as we prepare to begin our life together, we can’t wait to celebrate with all the people who’ve been part of our journey. Join us as we turn a childhood bond into a lifelong promise.
